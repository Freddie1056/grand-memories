About us

Nestled on the edge of Menengai Crater, Grand Memories Hotel offers a unique escape in Nakuru, with breathtaking views of Africa's largest volcanic caldera. Blending nature's beauty with modern comfort, our hotel provides the perfect mix of adventure and relaxation. Enjoy thoughtfully designed rooms, diverse dining options, and exceptional hospitality. Indulge in gourmet meals or casual bites, all while surrounded by stunning views. At Grand Memories, every stay is more than a visit—it’s a chance to create lasting memories in the heart of Kenya’s Rift Valley.
